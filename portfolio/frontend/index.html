(01)About

I'm an AI/ML developer drawn to the unglamorous part of the job — making models work outside the notebook, where real users, messy documents, and token budgets all push back.

At Vivansh Infotech, I build production RAG systems end-to-end: a multi-chatbot platform where users turn their own PDFs, docs, and crawled URLs into per-bot knowledge bases, and an AI medical transcription system that auto-generates structured forms from raw records. I care about the numbers behind the demo — I cut form generation to ~15s and dropped token usage 60% (25k → 10k) by re-architecting a two-stage LLM flow, and I tune the full retrieval chain (chunking, MiniLM embeddings in Pinecone, top-3 reranking) so GPT-4o answers from context instead of guessing.

Long before the job title, I was chasing problems for the fun of it: a Grand Finalist finish at the SSIP New India Vibrant Hackathon, Dhanvantari (an AI assistant for Ayurvedic consultations), and a sign-language translator that reads gestures in real time at 92% accuracy. I've completed my B.E. in AI & Data Science at GEC Rajkot — still building end-to-end, from backend to retrieval to the prompt that makes it all click.
